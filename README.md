# Project Name: EduALL Tutoring Dashboard 🎓

**Goal:**
Welcome to the EduALL Tutoring Dashboard! 🎉 This project aims to provide a super clear and easy-to-understand overview of our tutoring programs. Whether you're a student, a tutor, or just curious about how things are going, this dashboard is designed to give you all the important information at a glance. We want to make sure everyone stays on track and knows exactly what's happening with our tutoring services. Think of it as your one-stop shop for all things tutoring data! 📊

**Features each Page:**

### 📦 **Package Tracker (`index.html`)**
This page is all about our tutoring packages! Here, you can keep an eye on:
*   **Program Overview:** See which programs are most popular and how much revenue they're generating. 💰
*   **Package Status:** Track the status of each tutoring package, including renewals, upgrades, and trials. ✨
*   **Student Progress:** Understand how students are progressing through their programs, including those who are excelling and those who might need a little extra support. 🚀
*   **Revenue at a Glance:** Get a quick summary of our financial performance related to packages.

### 🧑‍🎓 **Student Tracker (`student.html`)**
Dive into the details of our amazing students on this page! You'll find:
*   **Active vs. Inactive:** See which students are currently engaged in programs and who has completed their journey with us. ✅
*   **Expiring Soon:** Identify packages that are nearing completion so we can reach out and offer continued support. 🔔
*   **Program & Curriculum Insights:** Discover trends in student program and curriculum choices with colorful charts. 🎨
*   **Comprehensive Student Data:** Access detailed information for each student, including their school, grade, and program specifics.

### 🧑‍🏫 **Tutor Tracker (`tutor.html`)**
This page is dedicated to our incredible tutors! Here, you can monitor:
*   **Tutor Activity:** See which tutors are active and who has recently updated their timesheets. ⏱️
*   **Workload & Timesheets:** Understand tutor workload and how many active timesheet entries they have. 📝
*   **Engagement Metrics:** Get insights into tutor engagement and contribution to our programs.
*   **Tutor Details:** View essential information about each tutor, ensuring everything runs smoothly.

**Conclusion:**
This Tutoring Dashboard is your go-to resource for staying informed about our tutoring programs, students, and tutors. We've designed it to be as user-friendly as possible, with clear visuals and easy-to-understand data. We hope this dashboard helps us all work together more effectively to provide the best possible learning experience for our students! Happy tracking! 🌟
